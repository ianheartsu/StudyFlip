~Reflection on Action List compared to actual deliverables~
When reflecting on deliverables, the project was not as efficient as it could have been. This due to the lack of frequent checking of the action list 
and more on what was completed during progress (work log). Learning from mistakes, in stage 3, there will be frequent reference to the action list 
and gantt chart to deliver close to the date finished. I also felt that the project was not as efficient as it could have been... Project should 
really be done in small parts over a regular basis and not at the end of the week to do a big part of it. I found that it would be better to 
do small parts and have them read for feedback (Agile Development).
